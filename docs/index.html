User Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to QuickWrite! This document provides an overview and instructions for using our app.

Signing up and Logging in

When opening the app, click on "Sign up instead," then input a valid e-mail and password then hit submit. The app should tell you to verify your email if done correctly.

After you go into your email and click on the link to verify your account, you can switch to the log-in screen by clicking on "Back to Sign-In" and putting in your credentials.

Creating a Username

After logging in for the first time, you will immediately be prompted to create a username.

In the text box in the middle of the screen, enter your username and make sure its in between 4-16 characters long, then hit submit

Answering the Prompt

Whenever you log into the app for the first time every day, you will be shown a daily creative writing prompt!

You will have 15 minutes to answer the prompt, type your answer out in the text box below the prompt then hit submit on the bottom left when your done

Anonymous submission

You have the option to submit anonymously by sliding the bar on the bottom left to the right. Your answer will still be displayed but noone will know it was from you.

The Home Screen

After answering the daily prompt, you will be navigated to the home page.

Prompt response

The home screen will show the response you gave to the daily prompt in the middle of your screen along with how many people liked it

Practice Prompts

By clicking on the practice prompts button, it will navigate you to a screen that lets you answer random prompts without a timer

To navigate back to the homescreen, click on the drop down menu on the top left of the screen and click on "Home"

View Others

Clicking on the "View Others" button allows you to view other user's prompts for the day.

Click "Next Response" on the bottom of your screen to move on to the next prompt.

Click on the heart on the top right of the prompt response to like a prompt, and click on the username to view their profile
